Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-21

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the daily-riddle gameplay and store your progress.

  • To maintain, secure, and improve the Service.
  • To respond to user questions or feedback.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers who help us operate the Service (e.g., our hosting provider) and only to the extent necessary.

4. Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at the address below.

5. Children

Riddledle is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Changes
